Chicken Shwarma Recipe

Dish Description

Chicken Shwarma is one of my favorite meals. It pairs fantastically with the other recipes on the home page. This will be a bit more involved of a recipe than the other two, but will absolutely be worth the effort.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb chicken thighs (skinless and boneless)
  • Pita bread or bread of choice to wrap it all in
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp ground cilantro
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp cardamon
  • 1 tbsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

Recipe Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients other than chicken and pita in a large ziplock bag. Add chicken and seal. Massage the bag to coat the chicken in the marinade and let rest overnight.
  2. Heat large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Drizzle with oil.
  3. Place chicken in skillet and cook 4 minutes to a side. After cooking move to plate and cover with foil to rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve chicken in wrap or pita of choice. Optional: prepare the Tabbouleh or Tahini Sauce recipes to add to the wrap.
  5. Enjoy!
