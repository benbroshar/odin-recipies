Tahini Sauce Recipe

Dish description

Tahini Sauce pairs exceptionally well with a wide variety of middle eastern dishes. It's pretty simple to make and goes a long way towards adding that special something to your dishes. This dish is much more of an art than a formula, so some ingredients do not have amounts. Instead, just add some, taste, and adjust.

Ingredients

  • Garlic cloves, pressed or minced
  • Tahini (about 1/2 cup)
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pinch of ground cumin
  • About 6 tbsp of water (relative to amount of tahini used)

Recipe Steps

  1. In a food processor, mix all ingredients
  2. Blend until mixed, and taste
  3. Add Lemon juice, salt, or garlic to taste. For best results add ingredients while pulsing or running the processor.
  4. Add more water to turn into more of a drizzle, or keep dehydrated for a thicker pour
  5. Pour onto your dish of choice and enjoy!
